Specialized Student Support

We offer enrichment programs for students in all different types of situations, from veterans to former foster youths.

  • ACCESS (Accessibility Resource Centers)
  • ACES (Upward Bound)
  • Arise (Asian American and Pacific Islander focus)
  • Aspire (African American focus)
  • Bridge (First Year Success)
  • CalWorks (Supporting those with Temporary Assistance to Needy Families)
  • DREAM (Undocumented Student focus)
  • EOPS (Extended Opportunity Programs and Services for Underserved)
  • Pride Center (LGBTQ+)
  • REACH (For Former Foster Youths)
  • Veterans Resource Center (For Vets and Dependents)

Student Health & Wellness

Health is crucial to your success in college, so Mt. SAC has resources for those who are housing insecure, food insecure, need medical support or a good workout. 

  • Basic Needs Resources (housing insecurity and more)
  • Mountie Fresh Food Pantry (food and support)
