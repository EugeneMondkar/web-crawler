      Emergency Management & Preparedness

      Mt. SAC faculty, staff, and administrators collaborate to provide as safe a campus and educational environment as possible. The Department of Police and Campus Safety, the offices of Student Life, Risk Management, Health Services, and Marketing work in concert to provide safety, emergency preparation, communications, and emergency services.
