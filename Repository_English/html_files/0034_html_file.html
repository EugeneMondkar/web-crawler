Mt. SAC MountieApp FAQs

 

    • On which devices does MountieApp run?
      MountieApp runs on the Apple iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch with iOS 4.X or higher and Android devices.
    • Where can I download it?
      Mt. SAC website: www.mtsac.edu/mobile/, the Apple App Store: http://itunes.apple.com/mountieapp, or Google Play Store: https://play.google.com/mountieapp/.
    • Can I register for classes using MountieApp?
      Yes. MountieApp allows you to search for classes and register for them by opening the desktop registration application in a new window on your mobile device. The MountieApp registration window is the same window viewers would come to if they clicked #4 on the Student Tab in the Portal.


Help us improve the MountieApp.  If you have comments, questions, or suggestions about MountieApp, contact us via email or complete the MountieApp feedback form.
