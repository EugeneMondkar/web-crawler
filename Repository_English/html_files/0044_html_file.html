Mt. SAC Access...

Mt. San Antonio College (Mt. SAC) is located 26 miles east of Downtown Los Angeles, on the eastern edge of Los Angeles County, in the City of Walnut, California. Mt. SAC is easily accessed from 3 major highways (10 FWY, 57 FWY, 60 FWY) and 5 major airports. Hilmer Lodge Stadium is located on the South East edge of campus off of Bonita Road.

Freeway Access

Mt. San Antonio College is located between the 10, 60 and 57 Freeways.

Coming from:

57 Freeway South (from Orange County)

  • North on 57 Freeway
  • Exit Grand Avenue
  • Turn left on Grand Avenue (west)
  • Follow Grand Avenue as it heads Northwest
  • Turn right on Temple Avenue (east)

57 Freeway North (From San Dimas/Glendora)

  • South on 57 Freeway
  • Exit Temple Avenue
  • Turn right on Temple Avenue (west)
  • Mt. SAC is just past Cal Poly

10 Freeway West (from Downtown Los Angeles)

  • East on the 10 Freeway
  • Exit Grand Avenue
  • Right on Grand Avenue (South)
  • Mt. SAC is three miles on the left.

10 Freeway East (from San Bernardino)

  • West on the 10 Freeway
  • South on the 57 Freeway
  • Exit Temple Avenue
  • Turn right on Temple Avenue (west)
  • Mt. SAC is just past Cal Poly

60 Freeway East (from Riverside)

  • West on the 60 Freeway (combines with 57 Freeway at Grand Ave.)
  • Exit Grand Avenue
  • Turn right on Grand Avenue (west)
  • Follow Grand Avenue as it head Northwest
  • Turn right on Temple Avenue (east)

60 Freeway West (from Los Angeles)

  • East on the 60 Freeway
  • Exit Grand Avenue
  • Turn left on Grand Avenue (west)
  • Follow Grand Avenue as it turns Northwest bound
  • Turn right on Temple Avenue (east)

Parking

A valid parking permit is required to park in all lots except for 30 minute parking spaces and metered parking.  Parking permits can be purchased at the Automatic Parking Ticket Dispensers ($4 for a one day permit) or at the Information/Parking Booths. Parking charges may be higher during certain special events. Mt. SAC Parking Lots are monitored 24 hours a day.  Anyone failing to adhere to Mt. SAC Parking Regulations will be cited. Please click here to visit the Mt. SAC Parking Map.
