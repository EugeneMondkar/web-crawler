Student Type Definitions

Continuing Student Any student who has enrolled in a credit class at Mt. SAC and received a grade during any of the last two regular semesters (excluding Summer and/or Winter Intersession).
Returning Student Any student who is coming back to Mt. SAC after an absence (i.e., did not receive a grade during any of the last two regular semesters or more).
New Student Any student who has never enrolled in credit classes at Mt. SAC
