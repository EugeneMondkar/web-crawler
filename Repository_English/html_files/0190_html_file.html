GIVING & INVOLVEMENT...

 
Many people don't realize that the Relays are a nonprofit organization, operated by the Mt. SAC Track & Field staff and a large, loyal group of dedicated volunteers. We invite you to join our growing list of partners and supporters of the Mt. SAC Relays through both financial and in-kind gifts and by volunteering your time to help with the Relays.
Here's a list of giving opportunities that can help you identify ways to support the world's finest track and field invitational. For more information, contact Special Events and Marketing Director Doug Todd at (909) 594-5611, ext. 4882 or 4840 or via e-mail: dtodd@mtsac.edu.
