        Mountie Fresh Basic Needs Resources

        The Mt. SAC Mountie Fresh Basic Needs Resources is a comprehensive campus-wide effort that serves students experiencing housing instability, food insecurity, and other emergency/crisis situations.                                        The program goals are to provide students in need with:                                                                                                 

        • Food Resources
        • Housing Referrals
        • Case-Management Support
                  COVID-19 Mountie Fresh Basic Needs Resources Updates:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Dear Mounties: To learn about the latest directives Mt. San Antonio College is taking in regard to the COVID-19 situation please visit the link here. 
        During these trying times with regards to the effects of COVID-19, we are working hard in trying to keep our website up to date with community resources that can provide some support as well as in adjusting our programming to provide both remote and in person services. Our CalFresh Outreach support will continue providing support remotely and in person and our consultations with our Basic Needs Resources Team can occur via Zoom (remotely) or in person. Please set up appointments using our Calendly scheduler located in the respective webpage. We are modifying our Mountie Fresh Food Pantry to follow campus directives and to keep our students and volunteers safe.
        Apply for CalFresh benefits from the comfort of your own home: Our partners at "Get CalFresh.org" have created a Mt. SAC personalized link for students who wish to apply for CalFresh benefits on their own and at their own pace!  This link allows you to complete a short 10 minute application to find out if you and your household are eligible! CalFresh Outreach staff from Mountie Fresh Basic Needs Resources are available to provide support at any point of the application process!   
        Community Resources: The search box below provides community resources depending on your geographical area. 
            Mobile Shower Access:    During COVID-19, changes have occurred to the mobile shower locations in Los Angeles County. Mobile showers and handwashing locations in Los Angeles County can be found here.
