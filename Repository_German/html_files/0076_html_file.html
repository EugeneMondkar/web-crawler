Research at LMU

LMU is one of Europe’s leading research institutions. With its highly diversified array of disciplines, it has outstanding potential for pioneering research.

A strong research environment

At LMU, researchers from all over the world encounter excellent conditions for their work — in their own research field and in interdisciplinary networks alike.

Studying at LMU

LMU has a reputation for its extensive range of subjects. Here, you can learn from leading academics at one of Germany’s most renowned universities. Study in the heart of Munich and gain first-hand, practical experience as you go.

Support every step of the way

Whether you're an international exchange or degree student, LMU always provides a helping hand. The University offers support in all areas of your academic and professional life.
