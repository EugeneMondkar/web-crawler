                  Detailed Directions

                  The Institute for Informatics has two locations, both are located in the city center and offer few parking opportunities. It is thus recommended to use public transport to reach the institute.

                  1) LMU main building
                  7) Theresienstraße 37-41: Lecture halls for most introductory courses, Department of Mathematics
                  8) Oettingenstraße 67: Main site of the Institute for Informatics
                  9) Amalienstraße 17: Site of  the units for Bioinformatics and Media Informatics

                  Further maps can be found at www.stadtplandienst.de.
                  Current public transport schedules are found at Münchner Verkehrsverbund.

                  Directions to Oettingenstraße 67

                  From the airport

                  • S-Bahn line S8 towards the city centre , exit at station Isartor
                  • Trolley line 18 towards Effnerplatz, exit at stop Tivolistraße

                  or

                  • S-Bahn line S8, exit at station Ostbahnhof
                  • Underground line U5 toeards Laimer Platz, exit at station Lehel
                  • Trolley line 18 towards Effnerplatz, exit at stop Tivolistraße

                  From the Central Station (Hauptbahnhof)

                  • Underground line U4 towards Arabellapark or U5 towards Neuperlach Süd, exit at station Lehel
                  • Trolley line 18 towards Effnerplatz, exit at stop Tivolistraße

                  From the Eastern Station (Ostbahnhof)

                  • Underground line U5 toeards Laimer Platz, exit at station Lehel
                  • Trolley line 18 towards Effnerplatz, exit at stop Tivolistraße

                  By car

                  • From the Mittlerer Ring take the exit "Tucherpark"
                  • take the second turn right into Tivolistraße
                  • take the second turn left into Oettingenstraße
                  • the  institute building is located right at the street corner, the main entrance is in the center of the building front.

                  Directions to  Amalienstraße 17

                  From the airport

                  • S-Bahn line S8 towards the city centre , exit at station Ostbahnhof
                  • Underground line U5 towards Laimer Platz, exit at station Odeonsplatz
                  • from there it is about 10 minutes to walk

                  or

                  • S-Bahn line S8 towards the city centre, exit at station Marienplatz
                  • Underground line U3 or U6 toeards Münchner Freiheit, exit at station Odeonsplatz
                  • from there it is about 10 minutes to walk

                  The trip takes about 1 hour.

                  From the Central Station (Hauptbahnhof)

                  • Underground line U4 or U5 towards Karlsplatz/Stachus, exit at station Odeonsplatz
                  • from there it is about 10 minutes to walk

                  From the Eastern Station (Ostbahnhof)
